# lokii.tech

**lokii.tech** is an open-source platform built by **lokii** (aka **UnEliteFish52**) with a vision to provide valuable lessons and guidance to break into Computer Science in today's competitive world.

## About

We are dedicated to **giving back to the community** by creating high-quality, accessible resources for aspiring developers, cybersecurity enthusiasts, and CS students. Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech professionals with practical knowledge and insights.

## What You'll Find

- Comprehensive CS notes and guides
- Tutorials on security, systems, and development tools
- Real-world practical lessons
- Community-driven contributions
- **Global Students Association (GSA)** - A complete mobile app for students worldwide
